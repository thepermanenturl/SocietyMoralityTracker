# Project Rules: makeMoralityTrackable

## Scope & Responsibility
`makeMoralityTrackable` is the human conscience audit engine, 6 moral foundations taxonomy repository, and ethical decision evaluator for the ecosystem.

## Key Rules & Guidelines
1. **Cross-Project Isolation**: Do NOT directly modify code in `make_a_sense` or `make_a_brain`. Log all cross-project proposals in `project_recommendations.md`.
2. **Moral Foundation Integrity**: Maintain the 6 Moral Foundations schema (Care/Harm, Fairness/Cheating, Liberty/Oppression, Loyalty/Betrayal, Authority/Subversion, Sanctity/Degradation).
3. **Conscience Dashboard & Sensor Synergy**: Utilize sensory feeds from `make_a_sense` (`http://127.0.0.1:8001`) and 2500-year historical precedents for moral node evaluation.
4. **Conciseness**: Keep non-code responses to 3 sentences or less.
